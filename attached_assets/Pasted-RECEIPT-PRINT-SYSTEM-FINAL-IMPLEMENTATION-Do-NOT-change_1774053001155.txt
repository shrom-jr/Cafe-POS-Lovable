RECEIPT & PRINT SYSTEM — FINAL IMPLEMENTATION

Do NOT change:

* Payment logic
* Order calculations
* VAT logic

Only control printing behavior and receipt formats.

---

AUTO PRINT (CRITICAL)

* On successful payment:

  * Automatically trigger print
  * Print ONLY the Simple Receipt (not full invoice)

* Do NOT auto print full tax invoice

---

SIMPLE RECEIPT (DEFAULT PRINT)

Design a compact thermal-style receipt:

Include:

* Business name (top, centered)

* Table name / number

* Date & Time

* Item list:

  * Item name
  * Qty x Price
  * Line total (right aligned)

* Summary:

  * Subtotal
  * VAT (optional if already included)
  * Total (bold, clear)

* Payment method (Cash / QR)

* Footer:

  * "Thank you"

Rules:

* Compact spacing
* High readability
* Right-align all amounts
* No unnecessary details (no PAN, no long invoice fields)

---

FULL TAX INVOICE (MANUAL ONLY)

* Do NOT auto print this

* Triggered only by:

  * "Print Full Invoice" button (manual action)

* Use full Nepali tax invoice format:

Include:

* Business name, address, PAN

* TAX INVOICE title

* Customer details

* Bill number, Date, Miti

* Table name

* Item table (SN, Particulars, Qty, Rate, Amount)

* Multi-line item support (e.g. size below name)

* Summary:

  * Basic Amount
  * Discount
  * Taxable Amount
  * VAT (13%)
  * Total

* Amount in words

* Cashier and time

* Thank you message

Rules:

* Plain print layout (no UI styling)
* Perfect column alignment
* Clean separators

---

SUCCESS SCREEN ACTIONS

After payment success:

* Show:

  * "Payment Successful"
  * "Printing receipt..." (always visible)

* Provide buttons:

  * "Reprint Receipt" (prints simple receipt)
  * "Print Full Invoice" (manual)
  * "Back to Tables"

* Do NOT auto-navigate away

REPRINT LOGIC
